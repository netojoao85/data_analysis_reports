The dataset variables (from a total of 12 variables) are span in 3 different types:
  • factor with character values;
  • datatime with data and time values;
  • factor numeric with integer and decimal numbers
In general the data is clean. Not all variables were used to answer the questions proposed, but three new variables were created:
  • month with the month that the bike was hired (January to December);
  • week_day with the week day that the bike was hired (Monday to Sunday);
  • station_travel join the start_station and end_station to have in just one variable the journey of each user.
Just "Male" and "Female" observations in the gender variable were considered. The "Unknown" observations were deleted.
The variable birth_age has an unexpected observation (1887). None action was took because this variable was not managed, but in the further studies this value should not be considered.
No missing values (NA) were deleted once it could delete useful observations. The NA values were managed (not considered) particularly when to make visualizations.
