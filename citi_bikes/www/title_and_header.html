Launched in May 2013 and available 24/7, Citi Bike, New York City's bike share system, boasts thousands of bikes and hundreds of stations, making it the largest in the USA with 143,000 members in 2018. There are available two types of services, the "Customers" with 24-hour or 3-day passes and "Subscribers" with annual subscriptions. Citi Bikes serves diverse needs, leisure, tourism, and even job commutes.
